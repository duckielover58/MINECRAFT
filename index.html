The Peaceful Mobs
The Peacful Mobs

Minecraft's docile mobs will NOT attack you even when attacked. They will simply run away. The mobs spawn very frequently during the day and mobs such as pigs, cows, and even chickens will spawn. If you kill them, you will get food. If you eat the food, you will make your huger bar go higher. If you make a furnace, and cook the food, you will actually get more food bars fill. You can also breed the mobs with their favorite food. For example, feed a carrot to a pig, then another to a pig nearby, they will find each other and will breed making another animal pop up. When your hunger bar is at max, you will start to gain hearts. But how do you lose them? ONTO THE NEXT SECTION!!

The Hostile Mobs
The Peacful Mobs

Minecraft's hostile mobs WILL attack you whether they got attacked or not. These mobs always spawn in the night, hunting you. They are always trying to kill you, and won't stop there. If they kill you near your spawn point, (which can be set by a bed or just where you spawned in the world) they will keep killing you. What's more, is that zombies and skeltons can pick up your items and use them against you. If you get hit, you will lose some of your hearts. If you want to know how to gain them back, look to the section above. Basically, these mobs are jerks. But wait, what about the mobs that don't attack unless attacked? ONTO THE NEXT SECTION!!

The Neutral Mobs
The Peacful Mobs

Minecraft's neutral mobs don't attack unless attacked. These mobs are usually the most dangerous. One of which is the enderman. Notriously known for its intense damage and teleportation abilities. The enderman will not attack unless you look at it in the eyes or hit it. The zombie pigman with a few other versions, all newer versions of pigman, won't attack until provoked. And when they are, they will ALL attack. Same with wolves, except, you can tame them with bones. Once tamed, they will fight alongside you and protect you.
