The Peaceful Mobs
The Peacful Mobs

Minecraft's docile mobs will NOT attack you even when attacked. They will simply run away. The mobs spawn very frequently during the day and mobs such as pigs, cows, and even chickens will spawn. If you kill them, you will get food. If you eat the food, you will make your huger bar go higher. If you make a furnace, and cook the food, you will actually get more food bars fill. You can also breed the mobs with their favorite food. For example, feed a carrot to a pig, then another to a pig nearby, they will find each other and will breed making another animal pop up. When your hunger bar is at max, you will start to gain hearts. But how do you lose them? ONTO THE NEXT SECTION!!
